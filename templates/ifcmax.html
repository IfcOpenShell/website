IfcMax: An ifc importer for Autodesk 3ds Max

Installation

  • Determine if your copy of 3ds Max is 32bit or 64bit
  • Download and unzip the appropriate version of IfcMax, move the unzipped file IfcMax.dli to your plugins folder, for example: C:\Program Files\Autodesk\3ds Max 2010\plugins
